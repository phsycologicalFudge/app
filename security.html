Our Security Approach

This page explains how we protect your data. It is written in plain language. If you have any questions, contact us and we will clarify further.

1. Protected Infrastructure

  • UK colocation facility with 24/7 monitoring, controlled access, redundant power, and cooling.
  • Physical security controls for server racks. Only authorised engineers can access hardware.
  • Planned capacity growth and redundancy to maintain availability during maintenance or faults.

2. Network and Access Security

  • Cloudflare protection in front of public services. Real server IPs are not exposed directly.
  • A dedicated tunnel and reverse proxy layer separates the public internet from core services.
  • Host firewalls and fail2ban help prevent common brute force attacks and block suspicious traffic.
  • Only required ports are open. Management services are restricted and monitored.

3. Data Encryption

  • TLS enforced for all connections. Modern ciphers and HSTS applied.
  • Encrypted storage on the server. If hardware is stolen, data remains unreadable.
  • Optional end to end encryption in the client for supported folders and files.
  • Backups stored separately with encryption and regular restore testing.

4. Account Security

  • Two factor authentication available for all accounts. We encourage enabling it, and can enforce it for admin users.
  • Role based access. Users are placed in plan based groups with appropriate permissions and quotas.
  • Login attempts are monitored. Repeated failures are automatically limited.

5. Compliance and Privacy

  • UK hosted and GDPR aligned. Your data stays in the UK unless you request otherwise.
  • No advertising and no data mining. Your files remain yours.
  • Clear privacy policy and terms. We explain what we collect and why, in simple terms.

Our Promise

No provider can claim perfect security. We follow a layered approach used by larger enterprises and continue to improve it. If you need a formal summary for your records, we can provide a short written statement of controls on request.
