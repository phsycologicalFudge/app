Security at ColourSwift Cloud

We built ColourSwift Cloud with security at the core. Here is how we keep your files safe, explained in simple terms.

Infrastructure Protection

  • Our servers are hosted in a secure UK facility with 24/7 monitoring, backup power, and cooling.
  • Only trusted engineers with clearance can access the physical hardware.
  • Systems are designed to keep running smoothly, even during maintenance or faults.

Network and Access Controls

  • We use Cloudflare to filter and block harmful traffic before it ever reaches us.
  • Connections go through secure tunnels and proxies instead of touching core servers directly.
  • Firewalls and smart blocking systems stop suspicious login attempts.
  • Only the ports we need are open, and admin access is closely monitored.

Data Encryption

  • All connections use HTTPS, the same standard banks use, so data cannot be read in transit.
  • Stored files are encrypted, so even if a hard drive was stolen, the data would remain scrambled.
  • You can enable end to end encryption for extra protection on chosen folders.
  • Backups are kept encrypted and tested regularly to make sure they can be restored.

Account Security

  • Two factor authentication is available for all accounts, and required for admins.
  • Access levels are based on your plan, so only the right people can reach certain features.
  • We track login attempts, and block repeated failures automatically.

Privacy and Compliance

  • Your data stays in the UK and follows GDPR rules, unless you ask us otherwise.
  • We never run ads, sell your data, or scan your files. What you upload stays yours.
  • Our privacy policy is written in clear language so you know exactly what we collect and why.

Our Promise

No system is perfect, but we use the same layered defences that large companies rely on, and we keep improving them. If you need a formal security statement for your records, we can provide one on request.
